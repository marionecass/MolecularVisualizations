More Information about the
[Tris(σ-diaminobenzene)Rhenium(VII)]+ Ion


The [Tris(σ-diaminobenzene)Rhenium(VII)]+ Ion (showing multiple bonds but not illustrating the delocalized nature of the bonds) is presented on your left. Each ligand carries a 2- charge. Three 2- ligands coordinate to the Re(VII) ion to create a complex with overall charge of +1.

Reference: A.A. Danopoulos, A.C.C. Wong, G. Wilkinson, M.B. Hursthouse, B. Hussain; J. Chem. Soc., Dalton Trans, 1990, 315.
