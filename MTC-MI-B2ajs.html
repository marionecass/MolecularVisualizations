More Information on
the Tris(1-hydroxy-2-aminoethane)Co(III) Molecule


The structure of the tris(1-hydroxy-2-aminoethane)Co(III) Molecule was caculated using Gaussian 98.
The ligand deprotonantes at oxygen and therefore carries a negative one (-1) charge. Three ligands are bound to the Co(III) ion to create neutral molecule.
