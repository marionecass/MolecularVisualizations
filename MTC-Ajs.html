A. What is a Metal Tris Chelate?

Metal Tris Chelates are an interesting class of (beautiful) molecules that have a metal ion attached to three bidentate (therefore chelating) ligands. The arrangement of the atoms produces a propeller shaped molecule.

Our first example is the tris(phenanthroline)ruthenium(II)2+ ion Ru(phen)32+ shown top left. (More Info / Reference)
