a-iii. Review of Method 1

As a review, on the top we show the Λ-Tris(acetonylacetonate)ruthenium(III) molecule
Potenially useful commands
Marion can wrap her fingers from back to front along a (any of the three) ligand backbone using her left hand with her thumb pointing towards you; therefore this is the Λ Isomer
You have only one thing to Remember: Lambda = Left
On the bottom we show the Δ-Tris(acetonylacetonate)chromium(III) molecule
Potentially Useful Viewing Commands:
Marion can wrap her fingers from back to front along one (any of the three) ligand backbones using her right hand with her thumb pointing towards you; therfore this is the Δ Isomer.
