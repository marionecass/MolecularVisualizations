More Information on the Cr(acac)3 Molecule

Many Tris(acetonylacetonate)metal complexes have been used in this Web site to demonstrate aspects of structure and symmetry.
The tris(acetonylacetonate)chromium(III) molecule is shown in two different views on your left.
The top structure shows multiple bonds but does not illustrate the delocalized nature of the bonds.
The bottom structure depicts the bonds as delocalized.
When deprotanated, each ligand carries a negative one charge and the 5 membered O-C-C-C-O ring is planar. The ligand is called acetonylacetonate and is abbreviated "acac".
